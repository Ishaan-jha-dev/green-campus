---

### Slide 1: The Vision
**Headline:** Empowering Institutional Net-Zero through Spatial Intelligence.
- **Project:** GreenPulse Campus
- **Team:** Ni-trogen
- **Mission:** Transitioning from "Manual Compliance" to "Automated Intelligence."

### Slide 2: The Problem
**Headline:** Institutional Carbon Blindness.
- **Fragmented Data**: Utility bills are disconnected from spatial reality.
- **Operational Leakage**: HVAC systems and phantom loads ignore occupancy schedules.
- **Audit Fatigue**: Carbon reporting is currently a manual, retroactive, and expensive process.

### Slide 3: Our Solution
**Headline:** The Unified Sustainability Operating System.
- **Native Telemetry**: Direct ingestion of smart-meter data.
- **Visual Auditing**: AI-enhanced spatial mapping of energy hotspots.
- **The Ledger**: A transparent, immutable history of every carbon-saving action taken by the institution.

### Slide 5: The Carbon Ledger
*(Ref: [carbon_real.png](file:///C:/Users/ishaa/.gemini/antigravity/brain/610100d7-196b-41fe-9303-5248081f7ca9/carbon_real.png))*
- **Tokenized Offsets**: Earn "Credits" (CR) for every kWh saved via intelligent scheduling.
- **Visual Breakdown**: Doughnut charts categorize impact (Solar vs. HVAC vs. Manual Action).
- **Audit-Ready History**: Transparent transaction log of all institutional carbon reductions.

### Slide 6: Institutional Energy Radar
*(Ref: [energy_real.png](file:///C:/Users/ishaa/.gemini/antigravity/brain/610100d7-196b-41fe-9303-5248081f7ca9/energy_real.png))*
- **Baseline Modeling**: Compare current usage against historical "Low Load" benchmarks.
- **Anomalies**: Detect equipment failure before it leads to massive energy spikes.

---

### Slide 7: Tech Stack & Scalability
- **Frontend**: Next.js 15, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion.
- **Data Engine**: Recharts (Custom D3 Wrappers) for high-fidelity institutional audit graphs.
- **Architecture**: Modular "Audit Nodes" capable of scaling from a single block to an entire university district.

### Slide 8: Why Ni-trogen wins?
- **Actionable UI**: Designed for administrators, not just data scientists.
- **Immediate ROI**: Reducing "Phantom Loads" can save an average campus 15-20% in energy costs in month one.
- **Scaling Potential**: Proven blueprint for smart, green campus transitions.
